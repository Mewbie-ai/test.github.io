Children English (CE)

Children English is a general English program ( General English ), especially for kindergarten and elementary school students . The program aims to foster their interest in learning English as well as develop their skills in Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing, & Grammar . With this comprehensive curriculum, students will be expected not only to cope with their English lessons at school, but also to speak and write properly, so that they can develop their personal and social life well.

English for children (CE) consists of 3 courses:

GE is a fixed program that takes place in 4 terms (period) a year, namely:
  • Term January - March (registration begins in December)
  • Term April - June (registration starts in March)
  • Term July - September (registration starts in June)
  • Term October - December (registration begins in September)
    • CTC is a program for elementary students (grades 4, 5, 6) and junior high schools aimed at fostering students' confidence in speaking . To achieve the above objectives, the teacher in the class emphasizes Fluency building by giving many opportunities for students to Listening ( language input ) & Speaking ( language output ).
    CEF is a Holiday English Program for Kindergarten (A & B) and elementary school students (grade 1, 2, 3, 4) held 3 x weekly @ 1.5 hours for 2 weeks (6 meetings) during school holidays, usually in June. In addition to giving students useful activities during long vacations, the program also aims to maintain the level of students' English proficiency that has been achieved so far. Because of the program's purpose for refresher, students in the class will be doing a lot of communicating activities, especially listening & speaking , which is interesting in English while playing.
